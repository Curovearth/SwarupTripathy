Space Applications Centre, ISRO, Ahmedabad, India

Onsite, Nov 2022 - May 2023

I worked on the project titled "Origami Inspired Wheel Transformation for the development of Robotic Applications" where me and my teammate designed and presented a 3D printed actuation system for the transformation of an origami inspired wheel structure.

Summer Researcher

Wolfram Summer School

Remote, Jun 2021 - July 2021

The research revolved around building a simple mathematical architecture for Conscious Turing Machines based on the paper "A theoretical Computer Science [TCS] Perspective on Consciousness" by Manuel and Lenore Blum. My project dealt with the implementation of dynamics of Conscious Turing Machines and rather than dealing with a conscious state. The project was assigned by Stephen Wolfram and was implemented under the guidance of Christopher Wolfram.
